In World Listener Script:               

This script is intended for in world builds not associated in any way with nPose (they are not a prop rezzed by nPose).  It will listen for color/texture commands on the channel arbNum for color/texture change link messages.

Dependencies:                               􀀓
Requires the attitional script nPose Color/Texture rev. .028.06 both in the base nPose build and the in world build.

Use:
Add the above listener and nPose Color/Texture rev. .028.06 or later version to items you desire to control textures and/or colors on that are not nPose props.
